-Streamdeck-

This my try at a Stream Deck. At heart, this is really just a custom keyboard, which can be used for many different things.

It could be used as a hotkey board, to easily mute/unmute the microphone or decrease/increase the volume during meeting. It could also be configured to open and close apps, execute a combination of keys to perform actions, like copy/paste, move between apps, etc.

Components

These are the main components used in the project:

  • 1 Arduino Pro Micro
  • 9 Sanwa Arcade Buttons
  • 2 Rotative Encoders
  • 1 Oled Display 0.96 (128x64)
  • 9 Leds
  • 9 220ohm Resistors

There are some additional components for future enhacement (which are not currently used):

  • 1 Buzzer
  • 1 Wi-Fi module
  • 1 Real Time Clock module

Schematics

In the picture, it is shown the whole schematics of the deck. It is not a complicated circuit. I guess the most complicated thing, was soldering and getting all the components and wires to fit into the box :)

Libraries

We are using some existing libraries for some of the components:

- Wire.h
- Adafruit_SSD1306.h
- Keyboard.h
